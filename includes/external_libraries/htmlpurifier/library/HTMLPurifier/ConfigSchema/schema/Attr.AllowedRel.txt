Attr.AllowedRel
TYPE: lookup
VERSION: 1.6.0
DEFAULT: array()
--DESCRIPTION--
List of allowed forward document relationships in the rel attribute. Common
values may be nofollow or print. By default, this is empty, meaning that no
document relationships are allowed.
